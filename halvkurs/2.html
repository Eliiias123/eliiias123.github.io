The Owl

The owl is often associated with wisdom, but in this image its stillness and vigilance are emphasized. Against the dark background, the structure of its feathers is clearly visible, creating a sense of both beauty and strength. The light captures just enough of its form to spark curiosity, while leaving enough in shadow to preserve the mystery. It is an image that allows the viewer to sense more than it shows, where the silence is almost tangible.
